Breath Relaxation

Did you know that you have a considerable influence on your feeling of relaxation through your breathing? With a simple exercise you can evoke a conscious feeling of relaxation. To do this, sit or lie down in a relaxed position.

Procedure "3363 method

Inhale slowly through the nose for about 3 seconds,
hold your breath for about 3 seconds,
Breathe slowly for about 6 seconds,
Hold your breath for the next 3 seconds, then start again.

Your pulse and level of activation will calm down and you will feel a sense of relaxation. You can practice this exercise at home and then use it in stressful situations.
